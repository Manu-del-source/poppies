Our Story

Founded in the vibrant streets of Nairobi, Poppies is more than just a restaurant. It's a culinary journey designed for the urban explorer. We believe in bold flavors, fresh local produce, and a dining experience that feels as alive as the city itself. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a celebratory feast, we welcome you to the family.
